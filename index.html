The studio model

Small portfolio, deep focus

Instead of one giant platform, we run a small group of independent products. Each one targets a vertical the incumbents underserve, ships fast, and stays small enough to operate sustainably.

Customer-funded

Every product is built to be cash-flow positive from its first paying customers. No venture capital. No growth-at-all-costs. No surprise pricing changes.

Real software

One stack across the portfolio: TypeScript, PostgreSQL, modern frontend frameworks, and our own cloud infrastructure. We own the code, we own the deployment, we own the customer relationship.

Long horizons

We build products we expect to operate for a decade. That shapes how we choose problems, how we price, and how we treat the customers who trust us with their data.
